About the project

A one-year initiative building lasting change

This project builds an interdisciplinary Community of Practice (CoP) — a group spanning disciplines, faculties, and strategic centres of the University of Bern. Together, the network develops an inter- and transdisciplinary working model addressing the opportunities and challenges of digitalisation in higher education, with a focus on Generation Alpha, the future students of this university.

Its goal is to place the human being at the centre of digital transformation. Across two semesters and a shared retreat, the CoP co-creates a common working model — the BRIDGE model — and a research agenda that prepares the University of Bern's teaching, research, and administration for the arrival of Generation Alpha, in line with UniBE's Digitalization Strategy 2030.

The CoP's findings and recommendations are consolidated in a White Paper.

The working model

The BRIDGE model

Developed by the Community of Practice, the BRIDGE model puts the human being at the centre of digital transformation. It bridges generations, the three groups that make up the university, and the transitions from school to university to the labour market — mapping the competencies each member needs along the way.
